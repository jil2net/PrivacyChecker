A small Android test app that lets you see what private information other apps on your device can access and collect (Radio Network/SIM, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, Accounts, Contacts, SMS/MMS, and Call Logs data).

## Functionality

* Shows radio, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth-related data
* Shows SIM-related data
* Shows account and sync-related data
* Shows contacts
* Shows SMS/MMS messages
* Shows call logs

All of the above data is displayed based on the permissions granted to the app.
